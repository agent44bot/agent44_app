ABOUT THE APP
Agent44 Labs is a control panel for AI agents that monitor websites and run automated tests for small businesses. The home screen shows a fleet of AI agents with live status; the agents power smoke tests, content monitoring, and AI-assisted social posts for paying customers.

All branding in the app — Agent44 Labs / Agent44Labs — is first-party. Agent44Labs is the developer's own brand and Apple Developer team name. There are no third-party brands represented in the app's user-facing surfaces.
